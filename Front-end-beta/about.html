About this App

A little bit about Reference Checker

The main purpose of Reference Checker is referencing improvement for medical research. A significant proportion of references in medical literature, are incorrect. This can be small errors in how they are written, or larger errors where references are omitted or do not support a statement.

Hence, Reference Checker is an open source forum that allows users to post any medical journals or articles which they think are referenced incorrectly to help improve the quality of research and help future readers of the article.
